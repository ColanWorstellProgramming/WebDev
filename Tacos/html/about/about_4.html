HOW IT’S GOING

Well, the mini tacos were a hit and Steve quickly realized he couldn’t keep
these just for himself. Using his background as a chef and knowledge
specialty food distribution he created a system to create, package, and ship
the mini taco shells fresh, in a variety of colors.

Using only the finest masa for the tortillas, each case of mini taco shells are
fried to crispy perfection for each order. As chefs, we understand the
imporatnce of freshness and provide our customers with nothing but
complete satisfaction.
